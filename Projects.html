A Airport Design by the founding members of Austro Club.

Siargao International Airport is a visionary project designed to connect the world to the breathtaking beauty of Siargao Island. Strategically located just kilometer away from General Luna, the airport is poised to serve as the primary aviation hub for this renowned tourist destination. With world-class facilities and cutting-edge technology, it aims to accommodate increasing air travel demands while ensuring efficiency and sustainability.

The airport features dual runways capable of handling a high volume of air traffic, making it an ideal gateway for domestic and international flights. A spacious terminal building with 32 aerobridges ensures seamless passenger flow, providing convenience and comfort for travelers. From efficient check-in counters to well-designed boarding areas, every aspect of the terminal is crafted to offer a hassle-free experience.

A Space Station Design by the founding members of Austro Club.

The Heavenly Cultivation Station is a lunar gateway concept that aims to facilitate human exploration and scientific research on and around the Moon. By acting as an orbital platform, it permits missions that require regular trips between lunar orbit and the Earth's surface, laying the groundwork for future deep-space missions. This station also aims to lay the groundwork for long-term human presence on the Moon, fostering scientific discoveries and giving a unique opportunity for in-situ resource utilization (ISRU) research and testing.

The Heavenly Cultivation will orbit the Moon in a near-rectilinear halo orbit (NRHO), providing consistent Earth contact by keeping within 1,500 km (930 mi) of the lunar north pole and up to 70,000 km (43,000 mi) above the south pole, with a 7-day orbital period. This configuration reduces communication dropouts and provides practically continuous control.
