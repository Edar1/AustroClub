The "Unveiling the Empty Space: Deep Space Asteroid Mining Mission Plan 'BELETHOR'" is a project that combines real-world and sci-fi themes which is this project's principal goal showing this mission’s primary goal to approach and take material from the asteroid. It also wants to know what kind of design to make. The spacecraft BELETHOR includes the SAT MCC, the central hub for sophisticated communication, observation, and analytic systems designed to house the Space Miner, Rocket DC, and Controlled Explosives that rely on effective communication and well-designed propulsion systems. Given its intricacy and relevance, the SAT MCC and other mission topics will be debated frequently.

Its rigorous and expensive development process has limited missions such as the OSIRIS-Rex, Hayabusa 2, and NEAR Shoemaker missions.
