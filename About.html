ABOUT US

Austro is an invite-only club with the sole purpose of sharing and promoting the member's projects.

We are a group of engineers passionate about designing and building, with a specialized background in aerospace that shapes and tailors our projects.

The club is a hub for like-minded individuals who share a passion for building and designing. It serves as a platform for you to share your projects, seek assistance, and collaborate with others who share your passion and enthusiasm.
